About Us

We are a compact, result-driven, proactive organisation going extra mile to exceed the expectations of our clients who we regard as partners.

Our strength is in competency based learning transfer which has endeared us to forward looking organisations Research has shown that growth and development are in separable ingredients in the attributes of all living species and organizations and that organizations which do not inspire her staff/workers to improve their knowledge, is most likely to experience the dual problem of dwindling efficiency and stunted capacity growth The benefits of growth capacity are derivable through training, retraining and practical experience. Grafa-Merit consult is involved in high powered human capital development in Nigeria offering local and International capacity building programme Our seminars provide participants with the latest knowledge based competency learning skills in a typical Nigerian environment and global terrains using global best practice methodologies which are positively experiential with focal points to the challenges faced in our working environment, we also provide toolkits for on-going measurement of operational performance.
